A tranquil night sky settles upon the troubled landscape of HASKELL CITY...
Ever since the disappearance of the beloved hero LAMBDA-MAN, in an epic battle to end all battles, his existence faded into myth and legend.
The city, meanwhile, descended into chaos and lawlessness, as malcontents swarmed her streets. 
Taking advantage of the power of HASKELL, the world's lifeblood, they schemed and plotted and waged war.
Haskell City's halcyon days were far, far behind her... but as we shall soon see, perhaps they can return.
For at this very moment, in the abandoned outskirts of the city, an adventurous youth exploring a cave system blunders into something he never expected to see...
