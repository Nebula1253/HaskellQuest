After defeating the dastardly Colonel, Lambda-Man managed to commandeer and land the airship himself.
(Though perhaps "managed to not die while crashing the airship" would be a more fitting description...)
A few weeks later, solidly established in his role as protector of Haskell City, Lambda-Man caught wind of a string of disappearances.
All noted researchers and scientists, all vanishing around the same time...
In his investigation, Lambda-Man found a potential suspect: Doctor Evelyn Kowalewski, the most prolific Haskell scientist in the city.
We join our hero now, as he breaks into Dr. Kowalewski's lab, only to be confronted by a shocking sight...
